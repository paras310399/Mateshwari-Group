OUR JOURNEY

2005

Incorporation & Pioneering the PPP Model

“Mateshwari Travels was established in 2005, initially launching with just 5 buses serving the Ahmedabad Municipal Transport Service (AMTS) under the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. This marked the company's early commitment to improving public transportation through collaboration with local authorities, setting a foundation for future growth.”

2006

Expansion to Jaipur Bus Operations

“A year after its inception, Mateshwari Travels expanded its operations to Jaipur, increasing its fleet size to 50 buses. With services now available in two key cities, Ahmedabad and Jaipur, the company began establishing a broader regional presence in public transportation.”

2013-2014

Tak Bus Operations Expansion

“In 2013, Mateshwari Travels continued its expansion, reaching Rajkot and increasing its fleet size to 250 buses. This period marked a major milestone in the company's growth as it extended its footprint across Gujarat and Rajasthan. By establishing operations in more cities, Mateshwari began solidifying its position as a key player in the regional bus transport sector.”

2015-2016

Achieving Best-in-Class Operational Efficiency

“By 2016, Mateshwari Travels had focused on optimizing its operations, becoming renowned for its operational efficiency and high-quality service delivery. The fleet had grown to 300 buses, serving Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and Rajkot. This period highlighted the company's dedication to service excellence, safety, and timely transport services.”

2020

Strategic Partnership with BEST-Mumbai

“In 2020, Mateshwari Travels took a significant leap forward by entering into a partnership with Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST), marking its entry into Mumbai’s public transport system. This partnership expanded the company’s reach to four major cities: Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Rajkot, and Mumbai, with a total fleet size of 480 buses, cementing Mateshwari's role in urban transportation across multiple states.”

2021-2022

New Business Initiatives and Continued Growth

“During this period, Mateshwari Travels initiated new business ventures, adding Udaipur to its list of operational cities. With a total fleet size of 700 buses, the company continued its trajectory of growth and diversification, catering to the increasing demand for public transport in both existing and new regions.”

2023-Present

Growth Decade & EV Expansion

“Currently, Mateshwari Travels is in its most transformative phase yet, embracing the next decade as a period of accelerated growth. The company has significantly expanded its fleet to 1,200 buses, with a major focus on introducing electric vehicle (EV) buses and modernizing urban transport facilities. This forward-thinking expansion aligns with global sustainability trends and the need for greener, more efficient public transportation systems.”
